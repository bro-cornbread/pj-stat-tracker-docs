Features

Everything a Pearl Jam fan needs

From complete setlist archives to personal concert diaries, stat dashboards, and a poster gallery.

🎶

Complete Concert Archive

Browse every Pearl Jam concert since 1990 with full setlists, set groupings, debut tags, and venue details. Filter by year to explore any era.

💎

Song Rarity System

Every song scored from Ultra-Rare to Staple based on performance frequency. Discover deep cuts played at less than 0.5% of all shows.

✅

Attendance Tracking

Mark shows as "I Was There", rate them 1-5 stars, and build your personal concert diary. See personalized stats based on your history.

📊

19 Stat Widgets

Customizable dashboard with bar charts, pie charts, ranked lists, and trend lines. 10 band stats + 9 personal stats widgets.

🏆

40 Trophies to Earn

Achievement system with drummer-themed tiers. Unlock trophies for attendance milestones, rare catches, geographic reach, and iconic venues.

🎨

Poster Gallery

Browse 1,039 show posters in a visual grid with year filters. Pinch-to-zoom on any poster. Images cached for offline viewing.

🎵

Song Lyrics

Read lyrics for 316 songs directly in the app. Collapsible display with "Show more" toggle on each song's detail page.

🎧

Album Deep Dives

14 albums with track listings, play counts, rarity badges, streaming links (Spotify, Apple Music, Tidal), and production credits.

📡

Offline Mode

SQLite cache stores previously viewed data for instant loading and offline access. Background sync refreshes when you're back online.

Song Rarity

Discover the deep cuts

Every song is scored based on how often it appears in setlists. A rarity score of 0.995 means it's been played at less than 0.5% of all shows.

Ultra-Rare
Played at <0.5% of shows — the holy grails
Rare
Played at <2% of shows
Uncommon
Played at <10% of shows
Common
Played at <40% of shows
Staple
Played at 40%+ of shows — the classics
